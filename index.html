Pianist, musical teacher, concertmaster

Igor Ivanov

I have been working in both the performing and teaching fields for many years. I have participated as a solo pianist, piano duo and chamber duo in various concerts and competitions. As a solo performer I played Beethoven Sonatas, Chopin Scherzos, Rachmaninoff Etudes, Ragtimes, Por una Cabeza, Gershwin's Rhapsody in Blue, some standards. Also, my students mastered the skills of performing solo playing and in duets, became winners of local and international competitions.
